Piotr Orłowski

I am a PhD Candidate at the Universitá della Svizzera italiana, Lugano. My research spans modeling of option prices and returns, high-frequency econometrics and corresponding option trading strategies, and applications of convexity bounds on stochastic discount factors.

My everyday work tools are R/Rcpp, SQL, and high-performance computation clusters.

I will be a candidate at the 2017 Finance Job Market.
